Paso 1. Crear Dockerfile para ms-citas
==============================================

WORKDIR /app
COPY target/ms-citas-0.0.1-SNAPSHOT.jar app.jar
EXPOSE 9090
ENTRYPOINT ["java", "-jar", "app.jar"]
